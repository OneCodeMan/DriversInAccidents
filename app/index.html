Drivers In Accidents

Using data taken from Michigan's Open Data Portal, We analyze information on drivers involved in car accidents. This project is based on social good, and its purpose is to raise awareness on the statistics of the people involved in these car accidents. In order to change something, we must know about it first. Analyzing data allows us to see correlations between variables, seeing what factors lead to the result of car accidents.
